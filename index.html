Architectural Inspirations

Explore the lives and works of pioneering women architects who shaped the course of design history.

Zaha Hadid

Known for her futuristic designs and radical deconstructivist style.

Lina Bo Bardi

Celebrated for integrating modern architecture with cultural context.

Jeanne Gang

Founder of Studio Gang, known for socially conscious urban design.

Elizabeth Diller

Co-founder of Diller Scofidio + Renfro, blending architecture and performance art.

Karen Cook

Part of the acclaimed firm Cook + Fox, emphasizing sustainable design.

Gae Aulenti

Italian architect known for museum renovations and adaptive reuse projects.

One of the first licensed female architects, worked closely with Frank Lloyd Wright.

Denise Scott Brown

Urban planner and theorist, influential in postmodern architectural thinking.

Odile Decq

French architect recognized for bold, unconventional designs.

Patricia Patkau

Canadian architect who collaborates with her sister on culturally sensitive projects.

Kazuyo Sejima

Co-founder of SANAA, known for minimalist, light-filled architecture.

Annabelle Selldorf

German-American architect famous for museum and gallery designs.

Julie Takahashi

Innovative architect and educator focused on experimental design strategies.
